A PLC-based Traffic Signal Controller with Timing Logic

üìò Project Overview

This PLC project simulates a four-road traffic signal using Siemens S7-300. The lights change based on programmed timers, ensuring safe and sequential traffic flow. The logic incorporates green, yellow, and red transitions with precise timing.

The project cycles through traffic phases as follows: Road 1 & 3 start green; Road 2 & 4 red. After 10s, Road 2 & 4 get yellow, and by 15s, they switch to green. Road 1 & 3 then go red. The entire cycle repeats with appropriate yellow transitions.

üü° Network 3: Yellow 2 & 4 ON (After 10s)

  • T4 starts at beginning
  • T5 triggers at 10s, enabling:
  • Q1.6 (Yellow 2) and Q1.1 (Yellow 4)

üü¢ Network 4: Green 2 & 4 ON (After 15s)

After T1 finishes:

  • Q1.4 (Green 2) and Q1.0 (Green 4) turn ON
  • Q0.2 (Red 1) and Q0.6 (Red 3) also activate

üî¥ Network 5: Red 1 & 3 ON

Ensures Q0.2 and Q0.6 remain ON while Road 2 & 4 are green.

üü° Network 6: Yellow 1 & 3 ON (Transition Back)

Q0.1 (Yellow 1) and Q0.5 (Yellow 3) prepare Road 1 & 3 to go Green again.
